Campus life

St. Lukes Day School SCHOOL-CAMPUS

St. Lukes is a ICSE Board school located in Naihati, Kolkata, India.The education is scientifically combined with tried and tested teaching methods, focussing on language development and logical thinking, for children to start their first steps into the education system.

ART CLASSROOM

To kindle a conducive learning environment on par with the best institutions worldwide, Chennai Public School has spared no effort or investment to ensure that the best infrastructure is made available to students to enable them to pursue their education with ease and comfort.

Smart Class – Interactive Digital classroom:

St.Lukes Day School was one of the first schools in India to have smart boards in all the classrooms and also a 5.1 theatre system in all classrooms.

Future 50 schools shaping success

St. lukes day School has etched a covetous place as one among the top 50 Schools in India, Shaping Success in the 2017 ranking conducted by the Future 50 - Fortune Magazine. Over 250 schools from 60+ cities all over the country participated in this ranking.

Sports & games
